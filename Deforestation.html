Deforestation

About 7,900 sq km (3,050 sq miles) of the world's largest rainforest was destroyed between August 2017 and July 2018 - an area roughly five times the size of London. New figures released on Friday by Brazil’s government show deforestation has risen by 13.7 per cent since the same time last year.

However during the past 5 years, satelite images shows the reduction of rainforest deforestation enough to lower heat-trapping emissions more than any other country on earth.
